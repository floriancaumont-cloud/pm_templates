# Problem Statement Template

## Purpose
Clearly articulate the problem before discussing solutions.  
A good problem statement aligns teams and prevents premature solutioning.

---

## Problem Statement

**Who is experiencing the problem?**  
> (User segment or persona)

**What is the problem?**  
> (Observed behavior or pain, not a solution)

**When / where does it occur?**  
> (Context, frequency, situation)

**Why is this a problem?**  
> (User impact and business impact)

---

## Evidence
- User quotes
- Data points
- Support tickets
- Observations

---

## Success Criteria
We will consider this problem meaningfully addressed when:
- [ ] User behavior changes (describe)
- [ ] Business metric improves (describe)

---

## Notes
- Avoid mentioning features
- Keep it observable and testable
