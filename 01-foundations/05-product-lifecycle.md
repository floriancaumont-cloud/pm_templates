# The Product Lifecycle

Products evolve, and so should Product Management.

## Common Stages
1. Discovery / Validation
2. Growth
3. Maturity
4. Decline or Reinvention

## PM Focus by Stage
- Discovery: Learning and validation
- Growth: Scaling value
- Maturity: Optimization and efficiency
- Decline: Strategic decisions

## Why Lifecycle Awareness Matters
- Prevents applying wrong strategies
- Aligns expectations
- Improves prioritization

## Key Takeaways
- Not all stages need the same tools
- PM responsibilities shift over time
- Strategy depends on maturity
