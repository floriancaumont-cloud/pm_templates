# Product Principles

Product principles are decision filters, not slogans.

## Why Principles Matter
- Scale decision-making
- Create consistency
- Reduce debate
- Empower teams

## Good Product Principles Are
- Clear
- Actionable
- Opinionated
- Few in number

## Example
> Prefer simplicity over completeness

This guides prioritization without micromanagement.

## Key Takeaways
- Principles shape culture
- They matter most under pressure
- Break them consciously, not accidentally
