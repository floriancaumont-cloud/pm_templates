# User Interview Guide

## Purpose
Guide consistent, unbiased user interviews focused on learning.

---

## Interview Objective
> What do we want to learn?

---

## Participant Profile
- Role / Segment
- Experience level
- Relevant context

---

## Warm-Up Questions
- Tell me about yourself
- Walk me through your typical day

---

## Core Questions
- Tell me about the last time you [did X]
- What was difficult or frustrating?
- How did you solve it?
- What happens if you donâ€™t solve it?

---

## Follow-Ups
- Why was that important?
- Can you give an example?
- What did you expect to happen?

---

## Wrap-Up
- Is there anything else we should have asked?
- Can we contact you again?

---

## Notes
- Avoid leading questions
- Focus on past behavior, not opinions
