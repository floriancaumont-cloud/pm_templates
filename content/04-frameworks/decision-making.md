## Purpose
Support confident decisions under uncertainty.

---

## Framework
1. Define the decision
2. Identify constraints
3. List options
4. Assess reversibility
5. Decide and communicate

---

## Good Practices
- Bias for learning
- Document rationale
- Revisit decisions when context changes
