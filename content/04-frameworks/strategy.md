## Purpose
Translate vision into focus.

---

## Framework
1. Define target segment
2. Identify key problems
3. Choose strategic bets
4. Define success metrics
5. Make explicit trade-offs

---

## Anti-Patterns
- Trying to do everything
- Strategy without metrics
