## Purpose
Reduce uncertainty before committing to delivery.

---

## Steps
1. Identify risky assumptions
2. Prioritize by risk
3. Design experiments
4. Run and measure
5. Decide next steps

---

## Outputs
- Validated assumptions
- Clear next actions

---

## Reminder
Discovery is continuous, not a phase.
