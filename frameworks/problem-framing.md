# Problem Framing Framework

## Purpose
Ensure teams are solving the right problem.

---

## Framework
1. Who is experiencing the problem?
2. What are they trying to achieve?
3. What is blocking them?
4. Why does it matter?
5. What happens if we do nothing?

---

## Output
A clear, testable problem statement.

---

## Common Pitfalls
- Jumping to solutions
- Framing business needs as user problems
