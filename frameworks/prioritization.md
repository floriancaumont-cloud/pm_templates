# Prioritization Frameworks

## Purpose
Make trade-offs explicit.

---

## Common Models
- RICE
- ICE
- MoSCoW
- Cost of Delay
- Opportunity Scoring

---

## Guiding Questions
- What creates the most user value?
- What aligns with strategy?
- What is risky if delayed?

---

## Reminder
No framework removes the need for judgment.
